About Poiesis

Inspired by the Greek term for "creation," Poiesis is a celebration of the inherent creativity within each individual. It's a vibrant platform for students to unleash their imagination, showcase their talents, and infuse every endeavor with a creative touch. Hosted by Sacred Heart Girls' High School, this festival is a reminder that creativity thrives within us all, waiting to be unleashed.

The Events

Terpsichore

The ultimate dance-off. Solos, duos, and group performances in Eastern & Western Categories.

Elysian

A musical paradise. Showcase your vocal and instrumental talents in solo, duet, or group categories.

Elixir

The battle of wits in Mad Ads. Create hilarious and imaginative ads for fictional products.

Entelechia

"Strut, Shine, Slay!" A fashion show celebrating transformation and style evolution.

Intaglio

"Sculpt beauty into every bite." An artistic fruit and vegetable carving competition.

Eikosmos

"A Universe of Perspectives." An immersive film review event for cinematic storytelling.

Eikonographia

Handmade poster making competition. Bring stories to life with paper and color.

Eminentia

The ultimate personality contest. Showcase your charisma, poise, and oratory skills.

Rules & Guidelines

General Rules

• Eligibility: Grades 9-12 & Undergraduates (1st-3rd year).

• Be present at Gate 20/21 (Residency Road) by 8:30 AM for registration confirmation.

• On-spot registrations close at 10:30 AM.

• Bags are subject to checking.

• Valid photo ID (school/college/government) is mandatory for entry.

• Entry stamps must be visible at all times.

Conduct & Attire

• Do not wear your institution's uniform.

• Inappropriate attire, conduct, vulgarity, or mockery will lead to disqualification or other serious action.

• Follow instructions from event heads and volunteers.

• Dress Code: Trousers and sleeved shirts only. Shorts, skirts, sleeveless, or ripped clothing is prohibited.

Restricted Items

• No weaponry (knives, scissors, etc.).

• No inflammable substances (deodorants, perfumes).

• No illegal substances (alcohol, drugs, vapes, etc.).

• No outside food, drinks, or water bottles.
